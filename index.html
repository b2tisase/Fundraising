Project Details

FUNDRAISING EVENTS

TBF also raises awareness for various issues affecting the people of Ethiopia by:

  • Individual donations
  • Corporate donations
  • Sale of merchandise: T-shirts, calendars, greetings cards
  • Sale of Ethiopian snack food at the multi-cultural get-togethers.

Awareness

  • Organizing discussions (Eye-on-Ethiopia discussion series is our future event)
  • Open house: Raise awareness for TBF among the local Ethiopian community and get feedback on TBF activities
  • Discussing social and economic developmental issues in TBF study group
  • TBF also acts as a network for various grass-roots workers and volunteers and non-governmental organizations.

Other activities

  • Fundraise for Ethiopians affected by natural disaster
  • Help new Ethiopian students joining each year
  • Trail maintenance.

Can you help TBF?

  • Become a TBF branch co-coordinator
  • Become a TBF projects co-coordinator: Help with project selection and their subsequent monitoring
  • Become a TBF volunteer: Attend meetings and help organize TBF events
  • Become the TBF-PROJECT donor and support a project
  • Attend TBF fundraising and awareness events
  • Contribute money to TBF.
  • Organize fundraising dinner for TBF volunteers and patrons.
